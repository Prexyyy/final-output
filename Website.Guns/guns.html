GUNS SECTION

In this section, you will learn about the different types of guns used in Valorant.

SIDEARMS

These types of guns are used typicallly in the first round of the game.

Classic

Usually bought in the first round. It has 12/36 bullets. The damage of the gun in 0-30 meters in head, body, legs, order is 78, 26, and 22. This is the cheapest gun in the game. The price of this gun is 0.

Frenzy

Usually bought in the first round. It has 13/39 bullets. This is the only pistol that is automatic. The damage of the gun in 0-30 meters in head, body, legs, order is 78, 26, and 22. The price of this gun is 450.

Ghost

Usually bought in the first round. It has 15/45 bullets. The price of this gun is 500. The damage of the gun in 0-30 meters in head, body, legs, order is 105, 26, and 22.

Shorty

Usually bought in first round. It has 2/10 bullets. This is the only sidearm that is a shotgun. The damage of the gun in 0-7 meters in head, body, legs, order is 24, 12, and 10. It has 15 pellets per shot. The price of this gun is 150.

Sheriff

Usually bought in first round or in a save round. It has 6/24 bullets. The damage of the gun in 0-30 meters in head, body, legs, order is 159, 55, and 46. The price of this gun is 800.

SHOTGUNS

These types of guns are used when playing close to a wall.

Bucky

Usually bought in ECO rounds. It has 5/10 bullets. The damage of the gun in 0-10 meters in head, body, legs, order is 40, 20, and 17. The price of this gun is 850.

Judge

Usually bought in ECO rounds. It has 7/21 bullets. The damage of the gun in 0-8 meters in head, body, legs, order is 34, 17, and 14. The price of this gun is 1850.

RIFLES

Guardian

Usually bought after first round or ECO rounds. It has 12/36 bullets. The damage of the gun in 0-50 meters in head, body, legs, order is 195, 65, and 48. The price of this gun is 2250.

Phantom

Usually bought after first round or ECO rounds. It has 30/90 bullets. The price of this gun is 2900. The damage of the gun in 0-15 meters in head, body, legs, order is 156, 39, and 33.

Vandal

Usually bought after first round or ECO rounds. It has 25/75 bullets. The damage of the gun in 0-50 meters in head, body, legs, order is 160, 40, and 34. The price of this gun is 2900.

SNIPERS

Marshal

Usually bought in ECO rounds. It has 5/15 bullets. The damage of the gun in 0-50 meters in head, body, legs, order is 202, 101, and 85. The price of this gun is 950.

Operator

Usually bought in buy rounds or after ECO rounds. It has 5/10 bullets. The damage of the gun in 0-50 meters in head, body, legs, order is 255, 150, and 120. The price of this gun is 4700.
